About

Muzaffarpur, a city in Bihar, is one of the cities selected for development under the Smart Cities Mission, launched by the Government of India. Here are some key points about the Muzaffarpur Smart City project

Gallery

Signification

  • Urban Mobility: Improving transportation systems, including roads and public transport, to ease congestion and pollution.
  • ICT Infrastructure: Promoting digital services for citizens, enhancing e-governance and providing access to information
  • Public Health and Safety: Creating safer public spaces with better healthcare services and improved emergency response systems.
  • Water Supply and Sewage: Ensuring clean drinking water and efficient sewage systems to prevent health hazards.
