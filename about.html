About

Muzaffarpur is a city located in the northern part of Bihar, India. It is the administrative headquarters of the Muzaffarpur district and one of the important cities in the state. Here are some key points about Muzaffarpur:

  1. Geographical Location
    • Muzaffarpur is situated on the banks of the Budha Gandak River, which adds to its natural beauty.
    • It lies in the northern part of Bihar and is about 75 kilometers north of Patna, the state capital

  2. Historical Significance
    • The city has a rich historical background and is known for its ancient temples and historical monuments.
    • Muzaffarpur is known as a part of the historical Mithila region, which was an important cultural and intellectual center in ancient India

  3. Education and Institutions
    • Muzaffarpur is home to several educational institutions like the Muzaffarpur Institute of Technology (MIT), B.R. Ambedkar Bihar University, and other colleges that cater to higher education in various fields.
    • It is known for its schools and colleges that offer quality education in different disciplines.
